Ricochet Radio

Brought To You By Sneak Peek Bangalore

Ricochet Radio is a dynamic one-hour radio show that airs every Friday or Saturday, bringing the heartbeat of Bangalore’s nightlife right to your speakers. Founded by DJ Wildrop, this show is dedicated to showcasing the latest trends in club music, featuring an exciting mix of trending hits, unreleased tracks, and emerging artists. As your go-to destination for discovering new sounds, Ricochet Radio embodies the spirit of the city’s club culture, inviting listeners to immerse themselves in the electrifying world of dance music. Now powered by Sneak Peek Bangalore, the show promises to keep you in the loop with the freshest beats and the ultimate party vibe. Tune in and let the music take you on a journey!
